kraken-js is a JavaScript runtime for Kraken OCR/HTR models by Benjamin Kiessling, running ONNX models in the browser via ONNX Runtime Web.

Known limitations: The bundled models are trained on Ancient Greek polytonic print; results on other scripts will be meaningless. Line detection uses oriented bounding rectangles (OBBs) derived from PCA — curved or wavy baselines are approximated as a straight rectangle, which may degrade accuracy on highly curved text. Region masking (excluding margins or illustrations from segmentation) is not supported; the full image is always processed.

The project Corpus Liberatum Linguae Graecae was supported by the French National Research Agency (ANR) under the France 2030 grant reference number « ANR-24-RRII-0002 » operated by the Inria Quadrant Program.
